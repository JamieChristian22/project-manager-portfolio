## 🎯 Business Problem
The organization faced several operational challenges impacting sales performance and efficiency:

- Limited visibility into the sales pipeline and deal progression  
- Manual reporting processes leading to inefficiencies and errors  
- Lack of centralized system for managing customer data and opportunities  
- Difficulty tracking performance metrics and business outcomes  

These issues reduced operational efficiency and limited data-driven decision-making.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
To address these challenges, an enterprise CRM system was implemented:

- Centralized platform for managing customer and sales data  
- Automated tracking of opportunities and deal progression  
- Integrated reporting and KPI dashboards  
- Agile-based implementation using structured project management
